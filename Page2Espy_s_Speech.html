NBA Tackling Race Issues

At the 2016 Espy Award show basketball players from the National Basketball Association, Carmelo Anthony,Chris Paul, Dwayne Wade, and Lebron James gave an powerful speech on Racial Injustice. Before the Espy's Carmelo Anthony, Chris Paul, Dwyane Wade and LeBron James together at the Microsoft Theatre in Los Angeles, urged their fellow athletes to be active socially.They talked about the violence and shootings that have happened and how racial profiling needs to stop.
